About Me

I am a Physics PhD graduate from Caltech with expertise in mathematical modeling, stochastic processes, and machine learning techniques. My research and industry experience span reinforcement learning, optimization, and statistical data analysis, with applications in AI, robotics, and finance. Looking ahead, I am also interested in exploring how deep learning methods can support environmental studies and wildlife conservation, bridging my scientific background with my passion for nature.

Outside of research, I am an avid wildlife and landscape photographer. Through my lens, I seek to capture the beauty and biodiversity of the natural world, telling stories that highlight both its fragility and resilience. Photography allows me to combine a precise, analytical mindset with artistic expression, reflecting my curiosity and appreciation for the connection between science, nature, and storytelling.
